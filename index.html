Where It All Started:

At the end of March 2024, my dad took my brother, Sam, and I to Matt's Offroad Games in Hurricane, UT. While watching Matt's Jeep XJ, The Banana, crawl up some intense obstacles, I was inpspired to build one of my own. My own car, a Subaru Forester, has had many struggles, and I had been considering buying different vehicle anyways. My dad and I made a deal that we would find one, rebuild it over the summer, and then I would purchase it from him before going back to school in the fall.
